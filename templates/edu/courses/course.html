Module 1: Understanding PTSD

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a mentalhealth condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. In crisis-affected areas, students are frequently exposed to violence, loss, and instability, increasing the risk of developing PTSD. Understanding PTSD is crucial for teachers to provide appropriate support and create a conducive learning environment.
